Introduction About Me

Hi! My name is Devi Izzati binti Safari, but people call me Dewi. I am 21 years old and living in Bukit Kapar, Klang, Selangor. I'm the third of four siblings, so being in the middle has taught me to appreciate different perspectives! I'm now doing a Diploma in Information Management, which I'm interested in because I appreciate working with organised and systematic materials. I'm a joyful person that enjoys learning new things and tries to be the greatest version of myself each day.

My Hobby

During my free time, I enjoy a variety of things that provide me joy and peace of mind. At home, I enjoy preparing various types of food for my family since I enjoy trying out new recipes and watching their joyful expressions when they test my dishes. I'm also interested about painting since it helps me to express my creativity and bring ideas to life via colours and shapes. To be healthy and refreshed, I enjoy outdoor activities such as jogging in the park and hiking. Being close to nature not only helps me keep my body healthy, but it also relaxes my mind and offers me a new perspective on life.

Education

I began my education at SK Bukit Kapar and attended religious school at SRA Bukit Kapar from the age of 7 to 12. Then I continued my secondary education in SMK Bukit Kapar, where I received 7 credits on the SPM test. After that, I joined in a Pre-Diploma program at UiTM Jengka for one semester before beginning my diploma. I got accepted into the Diploma in Information Management at UiTM Rembau, which is a course that I absolutely enjoy. After studying hard, I felt honoured to get the Dean's Award in Semester 4. From Semester 1 to 3, I participated in co-curricular activities such as the brass band and the colorguard team alongside to my studies. That taught me discipline, teamwork, and how to improve my academic and extracurricular performance.

Work Experience

After completing my SPM, I worked as an operator at Noripharma Sdn. Bhd. in Meru, where I was in charge of packing medicines and sorting defective tablets. During the Diploma, I internship at Hospital Al-Sultan Abdullah, UiTM Puncak Alam, in the Patient Information Management Department. Furthermore, I handled patient files, wrote medical reports, and learnt basic medical coding and statistics. I was also given the opportunity to develop the yearly report with Canva. This task gave me vital skills in teamwork, communication, and effective task management.
